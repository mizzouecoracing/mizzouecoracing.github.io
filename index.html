Mizzouâ€™s Electric Car Team and Hydrogen Car Team are now officially together under the Mizzou Eco-Racing Organization. The goal of the two teams is to promote zero emission vehicles throughout the community and teach students about each of these upcoming technologies. The Electric Car Team will be pursuing the Formula SAE Electric competition and the Hydrogen Car Team will be pursuing the Shell Eco-Marathon competition. The teams work together to complete each build to utilize each other's knowledge, resources, and skills.
